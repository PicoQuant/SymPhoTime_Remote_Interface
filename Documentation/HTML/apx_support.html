Support

    

We will continually make improvements and fix bugs where they can be identified. The results of these efforts will be incorporated into upcoming versions of the program.

When you plan to upgrade your license, please contact your local distributor or PicoQuant.

In any case, we would like to offer you our full support. Please do not hesitate to contact PicoQuant if you need assistance with your data analysis.

If you observe any errors or bugs, please try to find a reproducible error situation. Please e-mail us a detailed description of the problem and of any relevant circumstances, together with the data in question. Or use the Support Request formular at our website at   http://www.picoquant.com/contact/support. When you contact the support, please open the  Help | About  dialog, press the Get Support Information button and copy the contents of the window to a text file. Please attach this file when you mail your support request to: support@picoquant.com This will help us with the diagnosis of the problem. Thank you very much in advance. Your feedback will help us to improve the product and its documentation.

Of course, we also appreciate good news: If you have obtained exciting results or published a scientific paper, we would like to know! Please send us an e-mail containing the appropriate citation to info@picoquant.com. Gain additional publicity! PicoQuant maintains a database of publications mentioning PicoQuant devices and software. It can be found at our website at   http://www.picoquant.com/scientific. It is a valuable source if you would like to know which laboratories are using PicoQuant products or how broad the field of various applications is.

If interested in networking with other users of the SymPhoTime 64, or if you have comments or questions about the software, please visit our SPT-forum:   http://forum.picoquant.com/
